# Skill: Content Drafts

Go from idea to a usable first draft, grounded in vault material.

- **Trigger:** "Draft a [post/email/doc] about X."
- **Inputs:** topic; audience; channel; any source notes in the vault.
- **Output:** a note from `Templates/content-draft.md` in the relevant project,
  or `PARA/Resources/` if it's reusable.

## Steps
1. Nail down audience and the single key message before writing.
2. Pull relevant material from the vault; cite where it came from.
3. Outline, then draft freely — don't self-edit in the first pass.
4. Do a revision pass for clarity, length, and tone (per `Memory/preferences.md`).
5. Leave revision notes for the next pass.

## Quality bar
- One clear message; matches my voice; the reader knows what to do next.
