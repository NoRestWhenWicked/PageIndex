# Skill: Meeting Notes

Turn a raw transcript, rough notes, or a recap into clean, filed meeting notes.

- **Trigger:** "Write up this meeting" / "Summarize this meeting."
- **Inputs:** transcript or notes; meeting title, date, attendees; related project/area.
- **Output:** a note from `Templates/meeting-notes.md`, filed under the relevant
  `PARA/Projects/` or `PARA/Areas/` folder.

## Steps
1. Fill the template's metadata (date, attendees, related project).
2. Summarize the discussion into tight bullets — no transcript dumps.
3. Extract **decisions** explicitly.
4. Extract **action items** as `[ ]` tasks with owner and due date.
5. List follow-ups / open questions.
6. Link the note from the related project/area; add action items to the task list.

## Quality bar
- A reader who missed the meeting can act from the notes alone.
- Every action item has an owner.
