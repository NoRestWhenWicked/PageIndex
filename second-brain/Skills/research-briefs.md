# Skill: Research Briefs

Answer a question with a structured, cited brief instead of a wall of text.

- **Trigger:** "Research X" / "Put together a brief on X."
- **Inputs:** the question; any constraints (scope, sources to prefer/avoid, depth).
- **Output:** a note from `Templates/research-brief.md` in `PARA/Resources/`
  (or attached to the project that needs it).

## Steps
1. Restate the question in one sentence; confirm scope if ambiguous.
2. Search the vault first, then the web. Cite every external claim.
3. Break the answer into sub-questions; one findings subsection each.
4. Write the TL;DR last — 3-5 actionable bullets.
5. Note open questions and a recommended next action.

## Quality bar
- Claims are sourced; the TL;DR stands on its own.
- Distinguishes what's established from what's uncertain.
